User Guide

Welcome to UNI LIFE – Empowering Your University Journey! Here’s a quick guide on how to use the platform effectively:

1. Campus Map

This feature provides a user-friendly, interactive map of the university campus. It helps new students easily locate class buildings, libraries, cafeterias, and other essential facilities.

How to Use:

  • Click on the "Campus Map" tab from the homepage.
  • Zoom in on different campus areas like classrooms, libraries, and cafeterias.
  • Click on any building for additional information (location, working hours, etc.).

2. Budget Shopping & Halal Food Finder

Find nearby stores offering budget-friendly essentials and halal food options for an inclusive experience.

How to Use:

  • Enter your location or use your current location to find nearby stores.
  • The app will filter stores by budget-friendly options and halal food availability.

3. Student Finance Tracker

Track your finances by entering your monthly income and expenses, and get recommendations on how to manage your budget effectively.

How to Use:

  • Go to the "Finance Tracker" section to enter your monthly income.
  • Track your spending by categorizing each transaction.
  • Get monthly summaries and budgeting tips.

4. Direct Messaging to University Help Center

This feature enables students to contact the university’s help center directly for any queries or concerns.

How to Use:

  • Click on the "Help Center" tab to access the messaging system.
  • Select your query type (academic, personal, general).
  • Type your question and click “Send” for assistance.

5. Smart Chatbot for Support (NOT CONFIRMED)

A Smart Chatbot can assist with common queries, like course schedules, room locations, and campus information.

How it Works:

  • Access the chatbot through the "Chat with Support" button.
  • Ask common questions and get instant responses.
